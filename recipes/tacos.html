Tacos

These tacos are delicious and nutritious.

Here is how to make them for your friends or loved ones.

Or you can just eat them by yourself if you are alone in life.

Necessary Ingredients

You will need the following items

  • Prepared taco meat
  • Taco shells
  • oil
  • toppings
  • ingredients for margaritas

Recipe

This is the order you must use.

All others are bad.

  1. Heat your shells in oil on a skillet
  2. Once heated, fill with taco meat but not too much
  3. Cover the meat with the toppings.
  4. Separately, make a margarita.
  5. Consume the tacos while drinking several margaritas and maybe a beer.
